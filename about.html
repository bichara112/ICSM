Who are we?

● We are scientists and mathematicians whose goal is to create a stronger mathematical community and culture of mathematics accross the world, with emphasis on the developing world.

● We work closely with universities and research centers to facilitate access to graduate studies in applied mathematics and help improve the existing programs.

● We yearn to cultivate harmony between researchers, build bridges between the various axes of research in mathematics, and celebrate the common ground which consists in the understanding of natural phenomena based on mathematical rigor.
